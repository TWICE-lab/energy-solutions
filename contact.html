Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly for immediate assistance.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer free consultations for all our services. Contact us to schedule an appointment at your convenience.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Central Region of Ghana but can accommodate projects throughout the country depending on scope and requirements.

How long does a typical installation take?

Installation times vary by project. Small residential projects may take 1-3 days, while larger commercial installations can take several weeks.
