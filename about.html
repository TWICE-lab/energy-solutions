About VOLTIX

We're Ghana's premier TVET institute and technical services provider, transforming vocational education while delivering professional electrical and solar solutions. Our dual focus bridges the gap between quality technical training and industry needs.

David Kwabena Amenuku

Founder & Lead Engineer

With years of hands-on experience from industrial sites to family homes, David founded VOLTIX to bridge technical expertise with genuine care for customers. His vision is to make reliable energy accessible to all Ghanaians while creating opportunities through skills training.

VOLTIX was founded to address Ghana's critical skills gap in technical and vocational education while providing professional electrical and solar services to communities and industries. Our unique dual-focus model creates a sustainable ecosystem where training and services complement each other.

Our TVET institute provides comprehensive technical education that combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology, while our technical services division delivers professional electrical and solar solutions across Ghana.

What makes us different? We don't just teach theory - our students gain hands-on experience through real projects. We don't just provide services - we create employment opportunities for our graduates. We're building a brighter, more skilled future for Ghana.

Our Mission

To transform vocational education in Ghana through hybrid technical training while providing professional electrical and solar services to communities and industries.

For students, that means: No more education without practical application. No more skills without employment pathways. Just quality training that leads to real careers.

For Ghana's future, that means: Real skills for real people through hands-on training. Good jobs created by certifying the next generation of technicians. Stronger communities powered by sustainable energy and skilled professionals.

We don't just run programs. We build careers, empower communities, and drive sustainable development - one student, one project, one community at a time.

Our Vision

To become Ghana's leading TVET institute and technical services provider, recognized for excellence in vocational education and professional electrical solutions.

We envision a future where every Ghanaian youth has access to quality technical education that leads to meaningful employment. Where communities and industries benefit from reliable, professional electrical and solar services.

Our vision extends beyond just providing education and services - we're building a movement toward skills development, employment generation, and sustainable energy solutions across Ghana.

We see a Ghana where technical skills are valued, where vocational education transforms lives, and where sustainable energy powers progress for all.
