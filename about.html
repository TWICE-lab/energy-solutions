About Us

We're a team of passionate energy problem-solvers who believe reliable power should be simple, affordable, and sustainable. Founded by David Kwabena Amenuku - an electrical engineer with hands-on experience from industrial sites to family homes - we bridge technical expertise with genuine care for our customers.

David Kwabena Amenuku

Founder & Lead Engineer

With years of hands-on experience from industrial sites to family homes, David founded Davamen to bridge technical expertise with genuine care for customers. His vision is to make reliable energy accessible to all Ghanaians while creating opportunities through skills training.

What makes us different? We listen first, then design solutions that actually fit your life. We train local technicians because knowledge should empower communities. We stand by our work - from the solar panels we install to the skills we teach.

At our core, we're not just an electrical company - we're your partners in building a brighter, more energy-secure future for Ghana.

Our Mission

We're on a simple mission: to bring light, opportunity, and peace of mind to every community we serve.

For families, that means: No more sleepless nights over blackouts or sky-high bills. No more worrying about unsafe wiring or security. Just reliable power—clean, affordable, and tailored to your life.

For Ghana's future, that means: Real skills for real people through hands-on training. Good jobs created by certifying the next generation of technicians. Stronger communities powered by sustainable energy.

We don't just install systems. We build relationships and hope, one solar panel, one repaired wire, one trained graduate at a time.

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in solar and electrical services across Africa, improving energy access while promoting environmental sustainability.

We envision a future where every Ghanaian home and business has access to reliable, affordable power that doesn't compromise our environment. Where communities are empowered through skills development and local expertise.

Our vision extends beyond just providing services - we're building a movement toward energy independence and sustainable development across the continent.
